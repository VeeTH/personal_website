Who is Fukase?

Released: 2016 (V4)

Voice Provider: Satoshi Fukase

Fukase is known for his soft, emotional tone suited for a wide range of music genres, particularly ballads and electronic music. Fukase's voice is often described as clear, expressive, and slightly melancholic, making him popular for both heartfelt and experimental songs. His name and design were inspired by the lead vocalist of the band SEKAI NO OWARI. Fukase is regarded as a "memelord" in the Vocaloid community.

Who is Kagamine Len?

Released: 2007 (Act1), 2008 (Act2), 2010 (Append), 2015 (V4X), 2024 (SUPER PACK)

Voice Provider: Asami Shimoda

Kagamine Len is known for his youthful, energetic voice suited for upbeat pop, rock, and electronic music, though he can also convey softer, emotional tones. Len's voice is often described as powerful and expressive, making him popular for dynamic and emotional songs. His versatility and distinct character design have contributed to his lasting popularity in the Vocaloid community.
